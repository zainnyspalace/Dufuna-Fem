Turmeric Root & Powder

Native to Asia, this yellow Indian spice is a member of the ginger family it possesses a gingery-ness that’s often described as woody, musky, and floral & one of the highly prized spices in the world for its flavor and color. Turmeric often used as a dye for cloth, has become a staple for curries, soups, cakes and numerous other foods throughout Africa, Asia, and Europe. Ground turmeric powder imbues food with a peculiar flavor unique to turmeric.

Pepper Soup Spice

Pepper Soup spice is a special blend that contains a balance of spices, a fresh aroma & no additives native ingredients used in many African Nigerian soups and stews. Most of its components are difficult to find outside of Africa. Pepper soup is primarily liquid, usually served hot made by boiling Meat or Fish of choice. The broth from this Meat is then further seasoned with the authentic African spices. It has grown to become a stable Appetizer at events like weddings, & birthday parties.
